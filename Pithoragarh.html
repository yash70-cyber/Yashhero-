About Pithoragarh

Pithoragarh is a beautiful town in the state of Uttarakhand, India. It is often called the "Mini Kashmir" because of its scenic beauty, valleys, and snow-covered mountains.

Tourist Attractions

  • Askot Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Pithoragarh Fort
  • Kali Mandir
  • Chandak Hill
  • Thal Kedar

How to Reach

Pithoragarh can be reached by road from major cities like Haldwani, Almora, and Nainital. The nearest railway station is Tanakpur and nearest airport is Pantnagar.
